# References & Further Reading

**Homotopy Type Theory**

- The Univalent Foundations Program. *Homotopy Type Theory: Univalent
  Foundations of Mathematics* (The HoTT Book), 2013.
  <https://homotopytypetheory.org/book/>
- Cohen, Coquand, Huber, Mörtberg. "Cubical Type Theory: A Constructive
  Interpretation of the Univalence Axiom," 2018. (The CCHM paper.)
- Vezzosi, Mörtberg, Abel. "Cubical Agda: A Dependently Typed Programming
  Language with Univalence and Higher Inductive Types," 2021.

**Cohesive HoTT & Physics**

- Schreiber, Urs. "Differential Cohomology in a Cohesive ∞-Topos,"
  2013–ongoing. <https://ncatlab.org/schreiber/show/dcct>
- Schreiber, Urs. "Quantization via Linear Homotopy Types," 2014.
- Shulman, Michael. "Brouwer's Fixed-Point Theorem in Real-Cohesive
  Homotopy Type Theory," 2018.

**Holography & Entanglement-Geometry**

- Maldacena, Juan. "The Large N Limit of Superconformal Field Theories and
  Supergravity," 1997 (AdS/CFT).
- Ryu, Takayanagi. "Holographic Derivation of Entanglement Entropy from
  AdS/CFT," 2006.
- Van Raamsdonk, Mark. "Building Up Spacetime with Quantum Entanglement,"
  2010.
- Maldacena, Susskind. "Cool Horizons for Entangled Black Holes," 2013
  (ER = EPR).
- Swingle, Brian. "Entanglement Renormalization and Holography," 2012
  (MERA / AdS connection).
- Pastawski, Yoshida, Harlow, Preskill. "Holographic Quantum Error-Correcting
  Codes," 2015 (HaPPY code — a concrete toy model of holographic
  entanglement that is highly formalizable).

**Regge Calculus & Discrete Gravity**

- Sorkin, Rafael. "The Electromagnetic Field on a Simplicial Net," 1975.

**Tensor Networks**

- Vidal, Guifré. "Entanglement Renormalization," 2007 (MERA).
